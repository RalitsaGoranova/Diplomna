About Us

Welcome to Shared Travel, a pioneering platform dedicated to making travel more convenient, affordable, and environmentally friendly. Whether you're a frequent traveler or someone looking for a one-time ride, Shared Travel connects you with like-minded individuals who share your route and travel interests.

Our mission is to revolutionize the way people travel by offering a reliable, safe, and cost-effective solution for shared journeys. We believe in the power of community and the benefits of shared experiences. By choosing Shared Travel, you’re not just saving money, but also contributing to reducing carbon emissions and traffic congestion.

How it all began

Shared Travel was founded in 2024, inspired by the idea that travel could be more than just getting from point A to point B. We wanted to create a community-driven platform where travelers can connect, share experiences, and make new friends along the way.

Our Services

  • Shared Rides: We connect drivers and passengers heading in the same direction, making it easy to find a ride or offer one.
  • Easy Booking: Our user-friendly app allows you to book your shared travel quickly and securely.
  • Flexible Options: Whether you're planning a short trip across towns or a long-distance journey, Shared Travel offers flexible travel options to suit your needs.
  • Safe and Secure: We put your safety first. Our platform includes features like verified profiles and hiding your personal information to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all users. Only usernames , phone numbers and emails can be shared.

Getting started with Shared Travel is easy. Here’s a simple guide to help you use our app:

  • Create an Account: Registration page:Sign up using your email address and phone number.
  • Search for Rides:Search page: Enter your starting point, destination and time to find available rides that match your route. You can also filter results based on your preferences from landing page(Click the icon on top bar).
  • Book a Ride: Once you have found a suitable trip, click on "Choose".You need to write a short description (For example, how much luggage you have with you , whether you are travelling with a pet, etc.). After submitting the request you must wait for the driver to approve or decline your offer. You will receive an email with the response (Or you can check also on MyProfile page) and if approved you will receive an email and phone number of the driver to contact him and clarify the details of your trip.
  • Offer a Ride: If you’re driving, you can offer a ride by posting your trip details on create page.From the my profile page you can approve or decline offers , and when you receive an offer you will also be notified by email. Wait for passengers to book a seat.
  • Travel Safely: Meet your driver or passengers at the agreed location and enjoy your journey. Remember to leave a review after the trip!
